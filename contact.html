❓ Frequently Asked

Why isn't my music playing? ▾
Check your internet connection and make sure your browser allows autoplay. Try refreshing the page or clearing cache. If the issue persists, contact us!
How do I add songs to Favorites? ▾
Click the ❤️ icon on any track card to instantly save it to your Favorites library. You can access them from the sidebar anytime.
Is VibeSync free to use? ▾
Yes! VibeSync is completely free. We're building a premium plan with offline listening and exclusive content — stay tuned!
Can I request a song or artist? ▾
Absolutely! Use the form on the left and select "Feature Request". We read every suggestion and love hearing what our users want.
How do I report a bug? ▾
Use the "Report a Bug" link above, or fill out the form with Subject set to "Playback Problems". Screenshots are super helpful!
